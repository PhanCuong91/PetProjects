Issue 1: 
In case, entry price is equal ask price, cannot create a position and return 10015 (TRADE_RETCODE_INVALID_PRICE Invalid price in the request)
Root cause: Buy/Sell (Stop/Limit) is whether larger or smaller, but never equal
Solution: Cannot set type of order when ask price or bid price is equal entry price. verify the condition for a while, then increase entry price 0.5%

Issue 2:
Some SELL positions were cannot be created
Root cause: SELL position type shall be identified by comparision of BID price not ASK price
Solution: Use Bid price to comparision instead of Ask price
